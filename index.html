My name is Mallory and I am an innovative media creator with a passion for Interactive Design and Full Stack Development. My work bridges creativity and function, with a strong interest in UX/UI design. I’m passionate about building intuitive, engaging digital experiences, from concept to execution. I have concetrated my work into creating website and application designs that adhere to a high-quality user experience while maintaining accessibility, aesthetic, and functionality.

I’m always open to collaboration and learning opportunities—let’s connect!
